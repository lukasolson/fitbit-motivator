Fitbit Motivator

What's new?

Everything has changed in this update! You should now see a persistent icon next to the URL bar. (Clicking on it will take you to fitbit.com.) Different colors mean different things:

You're on track.

You're almost there.

You've got some work to do.

You need to get out and walk, like now.

If you're not on track, an icon badge will let you know how many minutes you need to walk, right now, to get back on track.
